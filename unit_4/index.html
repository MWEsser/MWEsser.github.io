Michael is a German citizen. He lives and works as a scriptwriter in Los Angeles. Michael has won the NEW YORK SREENWRITING CONTEST in 2014 and was a finalist at the LOS ANGELES FAMILY FILM FESTIVAL, the AUSTIN FILM FESTIVAL the same year and this year at WORLD SERIES OF SCREENWRITING, the LOS ANGELES SCREENPLAY CONTEST and STORYPROS. Michael has a first look deal with UFA FICTION in Berlin and works for other major German production companies in TV series development and as a scriptwriter.
